Demons

Demons are mostly evil beings of great power, not generally known to work together. Known to have enough power to the point where age does not affect them. They have power that ranges from barely killable by mortals to being able to rival the gods, and could technically “overthrow” the gods. Can be killed, but not forever. Demons are rare and usually plot their deeds in secret, with cultist-esc minions following them. Most are known to be okay with their positions as demons, very few have planned to overthrow the gods, and even less have actually attempted such. After being killed, they come back after a certain amount of time, and it seems that the weaker the demon, the faster they are revived.

Common demon traits: Truesight, (depending on age) proficiency in more languages than normal, A kind of “barrier” that blocks them from holy and celestial powers (such as gods smiting them or appearing before them), vulnerability to radiant damage and resistance to necrotic.

    • Ankris the Conqueror - most infamous demon, lich king and creator of The Legion, is the most powerful demon to have existed in recent history, and the closest to ever come to overthrowing the gods, even besting the accended hero Golmala before the rest of the pantheon overwhelmed him. currently out of commission

    • Minos the Disgraced - fallen hero and war crazed minotaur, wherever he appears, destruction is said to follow. Said to pop up every now and again. gained favor with goddess of power, a blessing he received after a great quest for her drove him mad and greedy for more power. Relatively new demon that has yet to be killed

    • Yorqiroth the Black Glutton - An old, old black dragon from very early in the world’s history. Very experienced in battle, is at his weakest when he first wakes up, because if he kills and consumes anything, he is able to take some of the power from that thing for a certain amount of time. Died a few times throughout history, currently out of commission

    • Ralmong the Demented Razer - Although his initial race isn’t definite, he has been theorized to originally be a satyr. Largely considered to be both the weakest and deadliest demon in history, he has died and come back several times throughout history, around once a generation, before being killed by a hero. He was previously a servant to another demon, faked loyalty in order to gain power before stabbing his master in the back. Has a very unique ability to shapeshift, although seems partial to a satyr-esque form when being “civil”. The lengths of time before they die again can vary, but there is always a hero to challenge them. Despite their status as being very weak, due to how often they revive, they have one of the highest kill counts of any of the demons. Additionally, not much is known about the heros who have managed to fell this demon. Currently Active, not many followers as he tends to murder them and prefers to remain mobile. In action as of recently

    • Animos the Twins of Hatred - This title actually belongs to two demons from long ago, and they took up the title of Animos due to their absolute hatred of each other. These two identical demons used to be a lone necromancer, however once he achieved demonhood he attempted to bypass the known “regenerative” state by cloning himself, resulting in what can be assumed as his soul either duplicating itself or splitting in half, creating two of the same demon, both hellbent on being the true “Animos” and looking for any way possible to put each other down for good. One currently In Action

    • Tyfónas the Elder Tempest - One of the four Elder Elementals, born of large storms in a large Air touched area upon her creation, This elemental was the most powerful of the four, but it was not enough, and the Tempest fell to dark means to increase her own power. When not dead, she rules over the area of her birth with a cold heart, and will sometimes even spread to other areas and increase her influence. Currently In Action

    • Agni the Were-chimera - She is the mother of all lycanthropes, and the leader of them all. Lycanthropes follow a specific hierarchy of devotion to the one who transformed them, but Agni is capable of controlling any individual one like an alpha for a pack. When she is out of commission her huge army is lead by her four generals, who are the lycanthropes she mostly controls. Generally has a following of lycanthropes rather than fiends, but that isn’t always the case. Currently In Action

    • Colimune the Mindless Consumer - a demon born of a failed experiment of the cult of the colimune, a cult which was basically trying to make the top leaders into demons, though it backfired, turning all the top people into an ever growing, ever consuming ooze. Has only been killed a couple times and has barely been seen outside of those times. Out of commission

    • Ar’or the Puppeteer - An extremely powerful and boastful Aboleth, it has managed to severely increase it’s mind-controlling abilities and magic. It has a very vast range and quantity of people that it can control, and multiple times it has managed to infiltrate governments and militaries, but has been foiled before damage went too far due to its boastful tendencies. It was once questioned by a group of heroes on it’s origins, but it only seemed to make Ar’or angry. Currently Out Of Commision

    • Vito the Profiteer - One of the weaker demons (which isn’t saying much) Vito is a (undecided), and tends to be more sympathetic towards people and a lot less destructive than most of his peers. He is mainly said to focus on maintaining his own wellbeing and fortune, and is one of the few demons to align himself in a more neutral position. After a lot of arguing and fighting Vito has managed to make a tenuous agreement with most of the world that he would provide the services of some of his servants through the use of Summon demon spells and magic contracts, in exchange for people leaving him alone. Additionally, he runs some other businesses both for people and demons, such as selling off his servants to other demons. He maintains a low profile and keeps his place of residence a secret for the most part, though he has recently been said to take up residence in Orestes. Currently In Action

    • Lorelei the Faceless Queen - A vampire demon that has reportedly never been seen by a living witness, not even via her own servants. Her army consists of an equal mix of fiends and vampire servants alike, and though her army is small and largely inactive, they are deadly and efficient when their queen calls upon them to act. Has never been killed
